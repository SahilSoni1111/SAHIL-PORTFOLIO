My Story

The Journey So Far

I am Sahil Soni, a Computer Science and Engineering student at VNIT Nagpur — one of India's premier National Institutes of Technology. My journey into technology began long before university, driven by a deep curiosity about how things work and an obsession with solving hard problems.

At Narayana e-Techno School, I wasn't just a good student — I was 3rd in all of Vidarbha Region in Class 10 with a 99% score, and followed that with 97.4% in Class 12. Academic excellence was never an accident; it was a deliberate choice made through consistency and focus.

The JEE journey was a testament to discipline. Cracking JEE Main with a 99.61 percentile and securing AIR 9169 in JEE Advanced opened the doors to VNIT Nagpur's Computer Science program — and I walked in ready to build.

At VNIT, I have found my purpose: writing clean code, solving algorithmic challenges, and creating software that matters. Every semester, every project, every late-night debugging session is a step toward becoming the developer I envision.

I believe in building in public, learning through projects, and surrounding yourself with problems slightly beyond your current ability. That tension between who you are and who you can be — that's where growth happens.
